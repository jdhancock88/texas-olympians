Texas gold

More than 100 athletes from the Lone Star State will compete in Brazil

By Jori Epstein | Staff Writer

Published July 29, 2016

Updated

    Texas will be well-represented at the 2016 Olympics in Rio de Janeiro, as over 100 athletes with ties across the state compete in Brazil.

    It might seem a little daunting to keep up with all of them. But you’re in luck.

    We’re tracking all athletes with Texas ties as they compete for their respective nations from Aug. 5-21 in Rio. Texas natives, current residents, anyone who went to high school or university in The Lone Star State? You can find them all here.

    The schedule below will update as athletes finish events with results, related stories and TV times. The athletes automatically sort alphabetically, but you can also sort by upcoming events -— so you can map out your calendar perfectly to cheer on Texans.

    Spot a Texan Olympian we missed? Email Jori Epstein at jepstein@dallasnews.com to tell us about omissions.
